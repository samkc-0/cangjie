# Product Overview

## What it is
A focused typing tutor for the Cangjie input method. It teaches the Cangjie roots, decomposition rules, and short phrases through structured lessons and rapid feedback.

## Who itâ€™s for
- Learners who want to build Cangjie muscle memory.
- Typists who need consistent, drill-based practice.
- Students who prefer short lessons and immediate correctness feedback.

## Core experience
1. Select a lesson.
2. Practice character and sentence drills.
3. Get immediate feedback and move forward automatically.
4. Track streaks and completion over time.

## Lesson design
- **Root lessons:** introduce the canonical Cangjie radicals and common combinations.
- **Rule lessons:** teach decomposition rules like left/right, top/bottom, and enclosure.
- **Sentence drills:** reinforce multi-character input and sequencing.

## Feedback and progress
- Instant correct/incorrect feedback on each drill.
- Streak tracking and per-lesson completion.
- Mastery tracking per character with spaced repetition intervals.

## Supporting material
- A learner guide (`GUIDE.md`) explains core Cangjie typing mechanics.
- A knowledge-graph spec (`CANGJIE-KG.md`) describes a potential mastery model.

## Non-goals
- It is not a full IME or character dictionary.
- It does not teach pronunciation or semantics beyond brief glosses.
- It is not a general Chinese-learning platform.
